Comparing Traditional Methods vs. Future Drone-Based Approach

Efficiency

Traditional Methods: Time-consuming, requiring extensive manpower and planning.

Drone-Based Approach: Rapid deployment, reducing time and resources needed for tranquilization operations.

Safety

Traditional Methods: High risk to both humans and animals, often involving close proximity interactions.

Drone-Based Approach: Enhanced safety by remotely managing the operation, minimizing direct contact and associated risks.

Effectiveness

Traditional Methods: Limited accuracy, higher chances of missing the target or causing unnecessary stress to animals.

Drone-Based Approach: High precision targeting, ensuring effective tranquilization with minimal stress to the animals.
